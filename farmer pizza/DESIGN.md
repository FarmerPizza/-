It moves away from the cluttered, high-energy tropes of traditional pizza marketing toward a sophisticated, editorial experience. The brand personality is confident, artisanal, and meticulous.The design style is **Minimalist with a high-fashion editorial edge**, drawing inspiration from luxury confectionery. It utilizes expansive whitespace to make food photography the hero, paired with aggressive typographic scales and a structured grid. The interface feels less like a utility and more like a curated menu, evoking an emotional response of quality, trust, and culinary indulgence.## ColorsThe palette is anchored by **Deep Charcoal** (#1C1C1C) for typography and structural elements, providing a heavy, grounded contrast against the **Dough Beige** (#F9F6F1) backgrounds.**Pizza Red** (#BD2F22) is used exclusively for primary actions, critical highlights, and brand signatures to maintain its punch and "appetite-triggering" utility. **Dusty Rose** and **Surface Gray** serve as secondary accents for muted UI elements like disabled states, secondary chips, or dividers. The overall tone should remain warm and organic rather than clinical.## TypographyThe typography strategy relies on the tension between the high-impact, condensed nature of **Anton** and the clean, precision-engineered look of **Hanken Grotesk**.* **Headlines:** Use Anton for all major headings. It should be used in uppercase to mimic premium packaging. For hero sections, use `display-lg` with tight letter spacing.* **Body:** Hanken Grotesk provides a modern, neutral balance that ensures readability for menu descriptions and nutritional info.* **Labels:** Use uppercase Hanken Grotesk with generous letter spacing (0.1em) for category titles, prices, and small metadata to maintain the editorial feel.## Layout \& SpacingThis design system uses a **Fixed Grid** philosophy for desktop to maintain a "lookbook" feel, while transitioning to a fluid, high-margin layout for mobile.* **Desktop:** 12-column grid with a 1280px max-width. Use 64px outer margins to create a sense of exclusivity and "breathable" luxury.* **Mobile:** 4-column fluid grid with 20px margins.* **Rhythm:** Use an 8px base unit. Section vertical spacing should be aggressive (120px+) to clearly separate product categories.* **Category Navigation:** Implement the signature horizontal scroll of circular containers at the top of menu pages, ensuring the active state is marked by a Pizza Red border.## Elevation \& DepthThe system avoids traditional shadows to maintain a clean, modern aesthetic. Instead, depth is achieved through **Tonal Layering** and **High-Contrast Outlines**.* **Surface Levels:** The base layer is `dough-beige`. Secondary containers (like cards or sidebars) should use `surface-gray` or `white`.* **Interaction Depth:** Instead of "lifting" an object with a shadow, use a 1px solid `deep-charcoal` border or a subtle shift in background color.* **Overlays:** Use a high-blur (20px+) backdrop filter for modals and mobile navigation menus to maintain a sense of space without adding visual clutter.## ShapesThe shape language is strictly **Sharp (0px)** for most structural UI components (buttons, input fields, cards) to emphasize the architectural, premium feel.**Exceptions:*** **Circular Category Icons:** These should be perfect circles to mimic the shape of a pizza and provide a visual "relief" from the sharp-edged grid.* **Avatars/Product Thumbnails:** If not full-bleed, these may use circular crops to maintain the "Simply Chocolate" inspired navigation language.## Components* **Buttons:** Primary buttons are sharp-edged, solid `pizza-red` with white `label-lg` text. Secondary buttons are outline-only `deep-charcoal`. Hover states should involve a solid color fill transition.* **Circular Nav:** Use 80px circles for categories. Images within should be high-resolution, top-down photography. Label below in `label-sm`.* **Cards:** Product cards are borderless with a `surface-gray` background. Images should be full-bleed at the top. Typography is left-aligned with ample padding (24px).* **Input Fields:** Minimalist 1px bottom-border only. Labels should float above in `label-sm`.* **Chips/Badges:** Use for dietary requirements (Vegan, GF). These should be small, sharp boxes with `dough-beige` background and `deep-charcoal` text.* **Quantity Selector:** A horizontal bar with "minus", "number", and "plus" separated by thin vertical 1px lines. No rounded corners.
